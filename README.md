# Landscape_Poster
xelatex landscape poster

## Guidelines for Neutrino 2026 Poster Preparation
### Format
Please prepare your poster in either A0 size (841 mm×1189 mm) or 4 ft×3 ft. All
posters should be in landscape orientation, no exceptions.
### Printing
Poster printing arrangements are the responsibility of each participant; the
conference does not provide poster printing services. There are FedEx and UPS
located within about a 10-minute walk of the conference venue where posters
can be printed. At both locations, 4 ft×3 ft is a standard poster size. UPS can
also print A0 posters, but this may require one business day.
### Fonts and Colors.

Please have the following general recommendations in mind when you make
your poster:
- Sans serif fonts
- Font size of at least 28 pt (all text should be readable from a distance of at least
2 meters)
- An off-white/pastel background to reduce glare
- A colorblind safe palette, which also makes your poster more visually appealing.
